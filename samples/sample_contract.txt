CONSULTING SERVICES AGREEMENT
This Consulting Services Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of March 19, 2025, by and between:

Tech Solutions Inc., a corporation registered in Delaware, with its principal office at 1234 Innovation Drive, San Francisco, CA 94105 ("Company"), and
John Doe Consulting LLC, a limited liability company with its principal office at 5678 Strategy Road, New York, NY 10001 ("Consultant").
1. SERVICES
1.1 Scope of Work: Consultant shall provide strategic business consulting services, including but not limited to market analysis, financial modeling, and business process optimization. 1.2 Deliverables: Consultant shall deliver all reports, recommendations, and analysis in a written format as specified by the Company.

2. COMPENSATION & PAYMENT TERMS
2.1 Fees: In consideration of the services provided, Company shall pay Consultant a fixed fee of $20,000 per month. 2.2 Invoice Submission: Consultant shall submit invoices on the first business day of each month. 2.3 Late Payments: Any unpaid invoices after 30 days shall accrue interest at a rate of 1.5% per month.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY
3.1 Definition of Confidential Information: Both parties agree that during the term of this Agreement, they may disclose trade secrets, customer lists, marketing strategies, and proprietary business processes. 3.2 Non-Disclosure Obligation: Consultant agrees to not disclose any Confidential Information to third parties for a period of five (5) years.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
4.1 Ownership of Work Product: Any intellectual property, including but not limited to documents, methodologies, and reports, created by Consultant under this Agreement shall be the exclusive property of the Company. 4.2 License to Use: Consultant grants Company a perpetual, royalty-free license to use any pre-existing materials developed by Consultant.

5. TERMINATION
5.1 Termination for Convenience: Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 daysâ€™ written notice. 5.2 Termination for Cause: The Company may terminate this Agreement immediately if:

Consultant fails to deliver services as agreed.
Consultant violates confidentiality obligations.
Consultant engages in fraud or misconduct.
6. GOVERNING LAW & DISPUTE RESOLUTION
6.1 Applicable Law: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California. 6.2 Dispute Resolution: Any disputes shall first be resolved through mediation, and if unresolved, shall be submitted to binding arbitration in San Francisco, California.

7. MISCELLANEOUS
7.1 Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties. 7.2 Amendments: No modification shall be valid unless in writing and signed by both parties. 7.3 Force Majeure: Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to natural disasters, government regulations, or other unforeseeable events.
